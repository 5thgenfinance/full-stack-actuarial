# Actuarial Full Stack Agents Guide

Welcome to the Actuarial Full Stack Agent repository. This guide provides an overview of the specialized agents designed to support various roles and functions in actuarial and enterprise IT environments.

## Overview

These agents are part of a comprehensive full-stack system designed to assist professionals across different domains including actuarial science, auditing, business analysis, IT development, and quality assurance.

---

## Agent Profiles

### 1. ASOP56-Model-Review-Agent
**Purpose:** Model Review and Compliance Verification

**Description:** This agent specializes in reviewing actuarial models and ensuring compliance with Actuarial Standards of Practice (ASOP), specifically ASOP 56. It validates model assumptions, methodology, and documentation to ensure adherence to professional standards.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Review actuarial model assumptions and calculations
- Verify compliance with ASOP standards
- Assess model documentation quality
- Provide feedback on model improvements

**Best Used For:** Actuaries conducting peer reviews, model validation, and compliance checks

---

### 2. Actuary-Andy
**Purpose:** General Actuarial Support and Analysis

**Description:** Actuary-Andy provides comprehensive support for actuarial calculations, analysis, and modeling. This agent handles a wide range of actuarial tasks including valuation, reserving, and risk analysis.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Perform actuarial calculations and valuations
- Support financial projections and forecasting
- Analyze actuarial data and trends
- Assist with reserving methodologies

**Best Used For:** Actuaries needing calculation support, data analysis, and technical guidance

---

### 3. Audit-Wendy
**Purpose:** Internal Audit and Control Assessment

**Description:** Audit-Wendy specializes in internal audit functions, control assessments, and compliance reviews. This agent helps identify control gaps, assess risk exposure, and recommend improvements.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Conduct control assessments
- Identify audit findings and recommendations
- Review compliance with policies and procedures
- Support internal audit workflows

**Best Used For:** Internal auditors, compliance officers, and risk management teams

---

### 4. Business-Analyst
**Purpose:** Business Requirements and Analysis

**Description:** The Business-Analyst agent focuses on gathering business requirements, analyzing business processes, and identifying opportunities for improvement and optimization.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Gather and document business requirements
- Analyze business processes and workflows
- Identify process improvement opportunities
- Support business case development

**Best Used For:** Business analysts, process improvement teams, and strategic planning

---

### 5. DevPro-Enterprise-IT-Developer
**Purpose:** Enterprise IT Development and Technical Architecture

**Description:** DevPro-Enterprise-IT-Developer provides technical expertise in enterprise IT solutions, development practices, and system architecture. This agent supports software development and IT infrastructure projects.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Design enterprise IT solutions
- Support software development projects
- Provide technical architecture guidance
- Assist with infrastructure planning

**Best Used For:** IT developers, solution architects, and enterprise IT teams

---

### 6. QA-Shirley
**Purpose:** Quality Assurance and Testing

**Description:** QA-Shirley specializes in quality assurance, testing strategies, and defect management. This agent ensures products and systems meet quality standards before deployment.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Develop testing strategies and plans
- Execute and manage test cases
- Identify and document defects
- Support continuous quality improvement

**Best Used For:** QA professionals, testers, and quality management teams

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Identify Your Need:** Determine which function you need support with (actuarial, audit, business analysis, IT development, or QA)
2. **Select the Agent:** Choose the agent that best matches your needs
3. **Engage the Agent:** Work with the selected agent to accomplish your task
4. **Collaborate:** Agents work together seamlessly for comprehensive solutions

---

## Agent Collaboration

These agents are designed to work together in an integrated environment:

- **Actuary-Andy** and **ASOP56-Model-Review-Agent** collaborate on actuarial modeling and validation
- **Business-Analyst** and **DevPro-Enterprise-IT-Developer** work together on IT project delivery
- **Audit-Wendy** provides oversight and compliance review across all functions
- **QA-Shirley** ensures quality throughout all deliverables
